Vector Word Representation For Lebanese news
Introducion

Below are a few tests for the word embeddings of the lebanese news. To see the results you have to write you input first and the press "submit". If you want to give your own words, they must be in the vocabulary of the word vectors. Our Embeddings are trained using word2vec algorithm with window size 10, embedding size of 250 and 1.32GB of lebanese news raw text.
